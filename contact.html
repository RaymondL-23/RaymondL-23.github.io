FAQ Questions

FAQ 1: What voltage are the power outlets?

FAQ 2: Can I get alcohol early in the morning?

FAQ 3: How old do I have to be to drink here?

FAQ 3: What language is spoken here?

FAQ Answers

Answer 1: Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as in the United States).

Answer 2: Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.

Answer 3: The drinking age on Taniti is 18 and the drinking age is not strictly enforced.

Answer 3: Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.
