Safety/Good Practice Info

  • Cruise ship arrives in Yellow leaf bay and is there for one night a week
  • The airport acommodates small jets and propeller planes, but will have a larger airport wiothin the next few years
  • Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day.
  • helmets for bikes are available to rent from several vendors (helmets are required by law).
  • Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing: this area is easy to explore on foot.
